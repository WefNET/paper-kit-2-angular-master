Server Setup

4x Skill Gain
4x Action Timers.

No fees for settlement creation.

8 hour farm ticks.

Start with 23 Body Strength and other chracteristics.

Server-Side Mods

  • Announcer: who's logging in and out. (And who's crashing due to lag ;)
  • Archeo-tweaks: Tweaks loot distribution to reduce junk-tier drops on "good" tiles.
  • Armor Stand: can change the model of you armor stand to reflect the actual armor stored.
  • Bag of Holding: A spell to increase the volume of a container.
  • Better Digging: Dirt drops on ground, also enables digging and other actions from cart.
  • Better Farming: Farm bigger and bigger area at higher skill. 30 Farming gets the 3x3 action, etc.
  • Bounty on Burn: Burning corpses grants 30 favor and 1 copper.
  • Crop Mod: Crops will not rot, and why would they?
  • Crafter Mod: Buy a crafter contract and skill up an NPC imper. 5% of the proceeds of a Crafter go to deed upkeep.
  • Discord Relay: Messages in our chat channels are relayed to Discord (and back again!)
  • Fire Burn Time: Forges, ovens and campfires display the burn time remaining on examination.
  • Gestation Mod: Adds a spell you can cast to reduce pregnancy timers for 1 day per cast.
  • Hitching Posts: tie your animals to something.
  • Location Command: /location tells you the X and Y of where you are on the map.
  • Minipets: Find eggs, hatch wee pets that follow you around.
  • One Tile Mining: expose a single rock tile with only four corners. Also, adds a rock leveling action.
  • Recall Runes: cast on runes and instantly recall to locations stored on each rune.
  • Scarfice Mod: 1 in 33 chance for a rare bone on sacrificing a rare item (at full favor).
  • Sindusk's Spellmod: massive quality of life improvement for priests.
    • No priest restrictions: everyone is a priest!
    • Up to 500 max faith and favor.
    • QL and rarity of the statuette to have added benefits to spellcasting.
  • Spellmod: all priests can can all spells!
  • Taxidermy: preserve your favorite furry friends and monsters.
  • Treasure Hunting: find maps from various actions. Use your compass to find buried treasure.
  • Vote Mod: Earn daily coin award by voting here!
